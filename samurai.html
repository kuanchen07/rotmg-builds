  • : Avalon's Intellect - Wisdom Tradeoff IV - Percent Mana Regen IV - Onability/Onhit Attack IV

We avoid going Relative Wisdom IV in this build since it doesn't scale up our Ring of Cubed Wisdom. In addition, having too much wisdom = too many shots on the wakizashi, and the main issue in the past with this build was that you couldn't hit all the shots on bosses even with the boss hitbox largening update.

For Wisdom Tradeoff enchants, avoid getting -attack, -dexterity, or -mana.
