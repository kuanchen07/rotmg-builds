Full Dex Paladin:

BiS Items & Swapouts:

Main Set: - - - or

Any ring with 8+ Dexterity on base, excluding enchants, for 's new breakpoint WITH on-hit/ability/Dexterity buff.

Swapouts: - (Keep using both seals.)

BiS Enchants:

  • : Hellfire Edge - Relative Dexterity IV - Dexterity Tradeoff IV - Onshoot Dexterity IV
  • : Relative Dexterity IV - Dexterity Tradeoff IV - Onability Dexterity IV - Mana Regen IV
  • : Relative Dexterity IV - Dexterity Tradeoff IV - Onhit Dexterity IV - Percent Health Regen IV
  • : Relative Dexterity IV - Dexterity Tradeoff IV - Onhit/Onability Dexterity IV - Percent Health Regen IV

The reason we go full into Dexterity is Hellfire on : Inflicts a poison within 4 squares dealing 750 damage over 3.6 seconds. Enemies under the effect of the poison receive 12% more damage from you, scaling with DEX. Damage boost is increased by 7% for every 10 DEX above 50.

On usage, it consumes 100 HP of your HP, so having percent health regeneration enchants can be nice at times.

For Dexterity Tradeoff enchants, avoid getting -attack.
