Mystic notes — Cult Staff boss hitboxes & Primal Arcana

Cult Staff: was recently buffed with a 100% hitbox increase on bosses, which makes it much more viable as a main staff than before — you can realistically land its shots without the old “kiss the sprite” positioning minigame on bulky boss hurtboxes.

Primal Arcana: also tends to feel better in boss fights where the target won’t sit still: its paired bonus shots are easier to consistently land together while the boss is moving, which stacks nicely with Cult Staff performing better once boss hurtboxes actually register hits reliably.
