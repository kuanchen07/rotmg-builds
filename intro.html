Introduction

As game balance continues to shift with each update, it’s important to stay up to date with which builds perform best at any given time. This guide focuses on the current best builds, providing a practical overview of what’s strong right now and how to make the most of each setup. I’ll be updating this regularly as balance changes roll out.

In addition to listing top builds, this guide also aims to help you understand the core concepts of creating your own build—covering things like:

  • Building around a specific weapon or ability
  • Choosing stats and enchants that fit your build’s scaling
  • Effective item swapouts for different situations

Whether you’re following the meta or experimenting with your own setups, this guide should give you a solid foundation to work from.

Building Around a Specific Weapon or Ability

When creating a meta build, the first thing to decide is which stat (or stats) you want to focus on to make your setup as strong as possible. Most of the time, Attack and Dexterity are the go-to, since they directly boost your weapon’s damage and fire rate.

However, if you plan to build around a different stat, like Wisdom or Vitality, you’ll need a solid reason—usually tied to how your ability or weapon scales. The main thing is understanding what part of your build benefits the most from additional stats and building around that.

For example, take the Full Wisdom Rogue, which currently has the highest single-target DPS in the game. Instead of stacking Attack or Dexterity, it invests heavily in Wisdom because its cloak’s scaling becomes incredibly strong as Wisdom increases. This justifies skipping traditional DPS enchants and items—though adding a touch of Dexterity still helps smooth out damage. (We’ll explore the Rogue in more detail later.)

Understanding Damage Stat Tradeoffs

Attack and Dexterity still matter for weapon-focused builds, but they are only part of the bigger picture. The best stat priority depends on what your weapon and ability actually scale from, how your build is meant to play, and which encounters you are preparing for.

Use stat balance as a guide, not a hard rule. For some setups, small shifts in utility or survivability stats can outperform pure damage stacking over a full fight.

Effective Item Swapouts and When to Use Them

Item swapouts are pieces of gear you can quickly switch to for a specific advantage — whether that’s boosting your damage or increasing survivability (which sometimes increases your DPS).
